About Us

Welcome to Universal Kworora Nursing Home (UKNH). We are dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of top-notch medical services. Our facility is equipped to handle Inpatient and Outpatient care, and we offer specialized services including Radiology, Laboratory tests, Dental care, Physiotherapy, Orthopedics, Eye care, and Pediatric services. We strive to deliver the highest standard of care to our community.

Our Services

Inpatient/Outpatient

Comprehensive care for both inpatient and outpatient services.

Maternity

Safe, supportive care for mothers and babies during and after childbirth.Manned by highly qualified professional staff with specialized certifications, UKNH maternity wing offer exceptional maternity services, obstetrics and gynecology specialized services. We have a state of the art hygienic facility that is well equipped.

Radiology/Imaging

Modern X-ray and scanning services for accurate diagnosis.

Dental

UKNH is fully fledged a modern dental specialty services.Dental consultation and oral health education, extractions, fillings (conservations), root canal treatment, crowns and bridges,scaling and polishing (cleaning),dentures/teeth replacement or artificial teeth, fracture reductions.

Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation services to help with recovery from injuries or surgery.

Orthopedics

Specialized care for bone and joint issues.

Eye Clinic

Comprehensive and computerised eye care services including exams and treatment.

Pediatrics

Dedicated care for children, from routine checkups to specialized treatments.

Pharmacy

At UKNH,we have an On-site pharmacy with a wide range of medicines and health products.Manned by highly qualified professional staff with specialized certifications, UKNH pharmacy prides in the quality of professional services offered to clients. The pharmacy is committed to providing effective services to both in-patients and out-patients, whilst ensuring that treatment is safe and appropriate. We dispense quality medicines at the best price possible. We employ qualified and experienced Doctors and Clinical Officers to ensure that our patients get the best medical advice and care. We have a fully fledged hospital Management System that improves on the speed of delivery of services. We also offer specialized clinics such as Gynecology, Dermatology, Orthopedic, Nephrology, Optical and ENT.
